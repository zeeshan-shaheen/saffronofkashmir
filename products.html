Saffron Literacy

How to Identify Real Saffron

85% of saffron sold globally is adulterated. Here's how to know yours is genuine — in three simple tests.

01

The Water Test

Place a few threads in cold water. Real saffron slowly releases a deep golden-yellow colour over 10–15 minutes. It won't bleed instantly — and the threads remain crimson throughout.

02

The Smell Test

Rub a few threads between your fingers. Genuine Mongra saffron has a complex, honey-like aroma with hay and metallic notes — never artificial or chemical.

03

The Shape Test

Each thread should be trumpet-shaped — narrow at one end, widening to a feathered top. Mongra grade has no yellow style attached. Any yellow at the base is a lower-grade Lachha or blended saffron.
