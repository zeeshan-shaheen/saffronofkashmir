Our Story

Generations of
Craft & Devotion
in Every Strand

Deep in the saffron fields of Pampore, where the Himalayan air is crisp and clean, our family has been cultivating the world's most prized spice for generations. Every single strand is hand-harvested at dawn — the only moment the Crocus sativus reveals its delicate crimson stigmas.

Our Mongra grade represents the absolute pinnacle of saffron purity — zero floral waste, only the deep red stigmas, dried over slow heat to preserve every molecule of Crocin, Safranal, and Picrocrocin.

The Origin

From Pampore to
Your Table

It takes approximately 150,000 hand-picked flowers and 40 hours of skilled labour to produce just one kilogram of saffron. Ours goes one step further — only the top Mongra stigmas make the cut.

01
Pre-Dawn Harvest Crocus flowers are hand-picked before 6 AM to lock in peak potency and colour yield.
02
Stigma Separation Only the three deep-red stigmas are separated by hand. Zero floral waste allowed.
03
Slow-Heat Drying Dried over controlled warmth to preserve Crocin, Safranal, and Picrocrocin — the triad of quality.
04
Sealed & Dispatched Sealed in your hand-painted tin within days of drying. Delivered to Dubai.

The world's finest Mongra-grade Kashmiri saffron. Family-farmed in Pampore. Delivered to Dubai with the care it deserves.
