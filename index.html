Our Story

The World's Finest
Red Gold
from the Valley

Deep in the saffron fields of Pampore, where the Himalayan air is crisp and clean, our family has been cultivating the world's most prized spice for generations. Every single strand is hand-harvested at dawn — the only moment the Crocus sativus reveals its delicate crimson stigmas.

Our Mongra grade represents the absolute pinnacle of saffron purity — zero floral waste, only the deep red stigmas, dried over slow heat to preserve every molecule of Crocin, Safranal, and Picrocrocin.
